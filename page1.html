About Julia

Hello, my name is Julia Arukakkal. I am a junior Computer Science major at Texas A&M, aiming for a career in technology and software development. Currently, I work in a genomics research lab where I apply my programming skills to analyze, build pipelines, and interpret large genomes. I enjoy unravelling complex problems and investigating different viewpoints with a team. In addition, I value strong communication & leadership skills through volunteering, teaching computer science, & leading women-focused organizations. I am eager to use my skills to build exciting and innovative technologies.
